How To Play?

Clash Of Communities - A community based pixel game where communities fight for huge pool prize!

To play placeit, every member must join a community or you can create your own community and invite new members in community to play the game.


Members of community can buy pixels on canvas to increase their share. You can buy any pixel on the canvas, even the pixels owned by other users to increase your holdings. Price for each pixel remain fix at 10 TRX.


All the income form sold pixels go into pool money. 70% of this pool money is the winning prize which goes to top community members.


At the end of every day GMT 12:00 pm, top community having the most pixels on the canvss wins the prize money with the following conditions.


a) Total pool money must be more than 7M TRX to get dividend distributed to the members fo winning community.

b) Minimum 100 x 100 pixels must be owned by the winning community


If any of the above conditions does not meet, pool money is carried on for next day, till both the conditions of winning the prize are met.


Every member of winning community gets the share of prize according to his contribution in the total pixels by community. To be eligible for this winning prize, you must stake 500 PLACE tokens before the dividend is due.


We welcome all of you into battle to claim highest number of pixels on canvas to win exclusive pool prize!
